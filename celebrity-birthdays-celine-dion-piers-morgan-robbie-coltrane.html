Celebrity birthdays 30 March: Celine Dion, Piers Morgan, Robbie Coltrane

August 2024 · 2 minute read

We're wishing many happy returns to these stars!

    Just before spring really hits in April, these celebs will be making the most of the penultimate day in March by celebrating their birthdays! From singing superstars to beloved 'magical' actors, we can imagine these birthday boys and girls will be marking their special days in very different ways, and we can't wait to see what they'll get up to. In the meantime, let's take a look at which celebrities we need to send our many happy returns to, what age they are turning, and what they have been up to in the lead-up to becoming another year older and wiser! Starting with…

    Celine Dion

    Celine is celebrating a landmark birthday this year by turning 50! The fabulous songstress has been enjoyed her Las Vegas residency over the last year, and we're certain she'll celebrate the important day in her own, spectacular way! Celine hit headlines earlier this year after waving away her security and expertly speaking to a woman who gate crashed her performance back in January. She told the audience: "You know what? Some people go through a lot, and some people need to talk. I want to say thank you to all of you, because for maybe five minutes we had given this lady a moment to talk."

    Celine will turn 50

    Piers Morgan

    Turning 53, Piers will most likely be enjoying a lie-in while enjoying his break from presenting ! The presenter took two weeks off while the studio changed venues, and spoke about how much he was looking forward to being in a new office. He said: "We got into the world's worst lift someone said to me, 'Are you sad to be leaving the ITV Studios?' And I said I'm about as sad as I am when I'm leaving the dental chair after having a root canal."

    Piers will turn 53

    Robbie Coltrane

    It's yer birthday Robbie! The actor turned 68. Since his stint as the beloved Keeper of the Keys, Rubeus Hagrid, in the eight magical films, Robbie has gone on to star in Brave, Great Expectation and National Treasure in a role that earned him a BAFTA nomination for Best Actor.  

    Robbie will turn 68
