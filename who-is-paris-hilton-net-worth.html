What is Paris Hiltons net worth? The US Sun

August 2024 · 3 minute read

BEING heiress to the Hilton hotel chain is just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to pinning down Paris Hilton.

The reality star and multi-millionaire married fiance Carter Reum at an elaborate wedding on November 11.

What is Paris Hilton's net worth?

Due to her several successful ventures and businesses, modeling career, and being heir to the Hilton Hotel empire, Paris has a net worth of approximately $300million.

She has handbags and perfume collaborations with the latter raking in a whopping $2billion in gross revenues.

Paris is also a DJ and has received as much as $250,000 from each performance, making her the highest-paid female DJ in 2014.

MORE ON PARIS HILTON

What is Paris Hilton famous for?

The hotel heiress first caught the media's eye after a sex tape was released in 2003 and she made her foray into TV soon after. Paris starred in the American reality series The Simple Life and won a Teen Choice Award for her role in the film House of Wax.

In addition to her acting career, she is also an author and released her first book, Confessions Of An Heiress, in 2004, which made it to the New York Times best-seller list.

Paris has continued to make her way in and out of the headlines over the years and starred in the documentary, This Is Paris, which premiered in September 2020.

How does Paris Hilton spend her money?

Property portfolio

Paris bought and sold her fair share of properties over the years, including a lavish $3million mansion in Hollywood Hills that she purchased in the early 2000s, around the same time she was on The Simple Life.

The home even made an appearance on the reality show, and it gave viewers a birdseye view of the comforts she enjoys.

Her dogs

The socialite's pets have also enjoyed her wealth as Paris had a two-story dog house designed and built outside her Beverly Hills mansion.

It is not the standard dog house with Paris going above and beyond to create some of the features. The doggy mansion includes upholstered beds, air conditioning, and even a balcony.

Her pets were not cheap to purchase either. She splashed $13,000 on her Pomeranian, Prince Hilton.

Fancy cars

Paris is apparently a massive fan of cars and has treated herself to Ferraris and Lamborghini's before turning to the pink Bentley Continental GT, which included a flashy $200,000 diamond-encrusted dashboard.

Some of Paris' other purchases include a Land Rover Range Rover estimated at nearly $100,000, a Rolls Royce Ghost believed to cost $324,000, and a Lexus LFA reportedly costing $350,000.

Is Paris Hilton married?

Paris started dating businessman Carter Reum in December 2019, and a little over a year later, Carter dropped to one knee and proposed while vacationing on a private island for Paris' 40th birthday.

The wedding takes place over three days, with the first kickoff celebration starting with the wedding ceremony and reception held on November 11. The couple were married at Paris' grandfather's estate in Bel Air and will celebrate with a carnival on The Santa Monica Pier in California, and will end on a high note with a black-tie event on Saturday.

Is Paris Hilton pregnant?

On July 27, Page Six reported that the glam queen was pregnant and expecting her first child with Reum; however, Paris seemingly denied those rumors.

“The only thing in the oven at the moment is my ‘Sliving Lasagna,’” Hilton said during an episode of her “This Is Paris” podcast.

Paris noted that she wanted to wait until after her wedding to conceive a child but already has names picked out.

She has chosen London for her future daughter but is keeping her future son’s name a secret for the time being.
