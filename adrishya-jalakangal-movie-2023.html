Plot

The Adrishya Jalakangal Movie revolves around the lives of three characters: a blind man, a deaf woman, and a mute child. The blind man, played by Tovino Thomas, is a former journalist who lost his vision in a bomb blast. He lives in a remote village with his mother and tries to cope with his disability. The deaf woman, played by Nimisha Sajayan, is a nurse who works in a hospital. She lives like a slave under the tyranny of her brutal and abusive husband. The mute child, played by Indrans, is a street urchin who survives by begging and stealing. He is constantly harassed and exploited by the local goons.

The movie shows how these three characters cross paths and form an unlikely bond. They find solace and support in each other, as they face the challenges and hardships of their lives. They also discover their hidden talents and potential, as they overcome their limitations. The movie portrays the struggles and aspirations of the marginalized and oppressed sections of society, who are often ignored and neglected by the mainstream.
