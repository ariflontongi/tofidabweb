FAQs

Does Coach offer free shipping?

Yes, at Coach, they offer free standard shipping on all orders over with no minimum spend. This typically takes 3-5 working days to arrive. Alternatively, if you’re after a faster shipping option, express shipping costs $13 for orders under $30 and $17.50 for orders over $30 and will take 2-3 business days to arrive. 

Does Coach offer a student discount?

Yes, at Coach, they offer a 10% student discount on select orders. Simply verify your student status through Student Beans to receive your exclusive student discount promo code to use towards your next purchase.

Does Coach have a welcome offer?

Yes, you can sign up for the Coach email newsletter to get 10% off your next order. Not only will you be able to make some excellent savings, but you’ll also be the first to hear about the latest Coach sale events, product launches, promo codes and offers, members-only events, and more. 

Is there a Coach Outlet store?

Yes, there is a Coach Outlet store. Although they are from the same company, we have a separate Coach Outlet promo codes retailer page. Coach Outlet stocks the same Coach bags you love at lower prices. 

Can I shop the Coach sale?

Yes, you can shop the sale all year long by heading to the ‘Sale’ page on the Coach website. In the past, customers have saved up to 50% off bags, shoes, jewelry, and accessories. Coach also runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, and New Year. 

Where can I find a Coach promo code?

If you're on the hunt for a Coach promo code, look no further. Marie Claire US always has a great selection of Coach promo codes available, so check this page if you're in need of one. Alternatively, refer to the Coach website and keep an eye out for any codes that will see you saving money. 

What is the returns policy at Coach?

If you’re not 100% satisfied with your purchase, Coach accepts returns made within 30 days of the date of receipt. Please note that all items must be returned unworn, unused, and with all tags, parts, and accessories. Please note that customized, made-to-order, leather care and fragrance products cannot be returned.
