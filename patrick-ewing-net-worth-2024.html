Early Life and Career

Patrick Ewing was born in Kingston, Jamaica, and moved to the United States at the age of 12. He quickly developed a passion for basketball and became a high school sensation. Professional Basketball Career

Ewing was the first overall pick in the 1985 NBA draft by the New York Knicks. He spent 15 seasons with the Knicks, earning 11 All-Star appearances and becoming the team’s all-time leading scorer. His professional career also included brief stints with the Seattle SuperSonics and Orlando Magic.

Coaching Career

After retiring as a player, Ewing transitioned into coaching. He has served as an assistant coach for several NBA teams, including the Washington Wizards, Houston Rockets, and Orlando Magic.
