Hayden Panettiere's Changing Looks

1998

The 9-year-old Hayden made her debut in animated film as the voice of Dot in A Bug's Life.

2002

Showing off her natural curls (and some adorable freckles!), the Ally McBeal actress walked the red carpet at the Movieguide Awards.

2004

Sans ringlets, Hayden wore a sleek updo to the Tribeca Film Festival screening of Raising Helen.

2006

At the premiere of The Architect, Hayden's hair was all about beachy blond waves.

2007

The Heroes star was red carpet-ready at the Vanity Fair Oscars party in a textured side bun and luscious lashes.

2007

Panettiere added interest to her stick-straight locks by pinning back a section at the crown.

2008

The actress showed off her new bangs and face-framing layers at a charity benefit.

2008

Hayden complemented her edgy razored bob with red patent lips.

2009

The star of I Love You Beth Cooper arrived at the Teen Choice Awards in an undone updo and bubblegum pink lips.

2009

"I wanted to give her Hollywood glamour, but keep the look young and sophisticated," hairstylist Craig Gangi said of Panettiere's curly Emmys updo.

2010

Panettiere was barely recognizable (but equally stunning) as a brunette.

2010

Hayden switched up her style once again, turning heads with an angled pixie.

2012

A sleek coif, matte red lips and long lashes for the Elton John AIDS Foundation Oscars viewing party.

2012

The Nashville star went long for the ABC upfronts.

2012

Panettiere hit the CMT Music Awards sporting swept-up curls.

2013

Panettiere opts for a sleek hairstyle and bold brows.

2014

The star debuts her new brow-grazing fringe and new hair hue.

2016

Panettiere showed off a new wavy lob at the Critics' Choice Awards, pairing the beachy style with a glossy pink lip.
