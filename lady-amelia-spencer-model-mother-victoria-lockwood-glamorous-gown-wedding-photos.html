Lady Amelia Spencer's model mother Victoria Lockwood looks incredible in sweet wedding photos

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Exclusive photos inside Princess Diana's niece's wedding

    Lady Amelia Spencer's family dressed to the nines for her wedding day with Greg Mallett, from her twin sister Lady Eliza's pretty pink maid of honour gown to her brother Samuel's dapper black suit.

    But did you spot her glamorous mother Victoria Lockwood? The former model, 57, was one of 122 guests who gathered in South Africa for the nuptials on 21 March. She looked stunning in a silky sleeveless gown in a rich teal colour, complete with a ruched neckline and a fluted midi skirt that showed off her bronzed glow.

    Victoria, who was previously married to Amelia's father Charles Spencer from 1989 to 1997, offset her mother-of-the-bride outfit with sparkly drop earrings and a matching bracelet, alongside pale blue shoes and a black bag. 

    In pictures shared exclusively with HELLO!, Victoria walked down the aisle at Quoin Rock Manor House in South Africa’s Western Cape alongside Greg's mother, who wore a black sparkly jumpsuit. She later posed with her daughter against the backdrop of the mountains, showing off the bride's custom gown by Versace, a designer loved by Amelia's late aunt Princess Diana.

    Crafted over two and a half months, the gown featured a two-and-a-half metre train, Swarovski crystals, a hand-embroidered neckline and structured shoulders, with intricate flower patterns running throughout. Prince William's cousin added an elegant five-metre-long veil made out of fine silk tulle and Jimmy Choo heels.

    EXCLUSIVE: Lady Amelia Spencer's emotional moment over corset wedding dress

    Recalling the emotional moment she saw her dress in person for the first time, during one of her fittings, for which she was joined by twin sister Lady Eliza and older sister Lady Kitty, Amelia said: "My eyes filled up [with tears], so did my sisters’. When I put it on, I was just so happy and grateful that it was my dream dress, I didn't change one thing. It fit like a glove."

    Meanwhile, Eliza and Kitty's gowns were also Atelier Versace creations – the former wore a high-neck pink satin dress with a strappy back and a leg split, while the latter wore a Grecian pale blue one-shouldered dress.

    Both kept their blonde hair down in glamorous waves, while Amelia secured hers in a sleek bun finished with embellished accessories.
