August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Warning: This story contains spoilers for Game of Thrones Season 7 Episode 6 "Beyond the Wall."

Game of Thrones has boasted an impressive wardrobe since the beginning, but it's been a while since a jaw-dropping look has graced the screen. Cersei hasn't changed out of that studded charcoal number, Sansa wears the same dark furs and Daenerys, who's donned some intricate dresses throughout the series, has only made subtle wardrobe changes in the same neutral color scheme this season. Plus, with the Martells and Tyrells out of the picture, we're missing out on a lot of major gown moments.

But that all changed last night when Dany unexpectedly changed into a fur-lined coatdress for her trip north of the Wall.

The look featured striped white and gray panels and a crossbody chain with the Targaryen three-headed dragon sigil.
