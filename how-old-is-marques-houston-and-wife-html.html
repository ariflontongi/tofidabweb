Marques Houston and Wife: Age is Just a Number (But Maybe a Hilarious One)

Marques Houston, the smooth R&B crooner we all know and love from back in the Immature days (remember "Damn!") is back in the headlines, but this time it's not about a steamy new single. It's about his wife, Miya, and let's just say, the age difference has the internet buzzing like a beehive at a Beyoncé concert.

So, how old is Marques Houston?

The man himself was born in 1981, making him 42 years old as of today. Still killing it in the ageless R&B singer department, that's for sure.

And his wife, Miya?

Well, this is where things get interesting. Miya is reported to be around 22 years old. Now, before you grab your calculator and start questioning time travel or the Benjamin Button effect, let's just say it's a noticeable age gap.

May-December Romance or More Like May-December-And-Then-Some?

The important thing here is that Marques and Miya seem happy, and hey, age ain't nothing but a number...as long as you both can agree on what decade it is for movie night.
