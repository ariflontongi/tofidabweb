Did you spot this Friends star in Death in Paradise?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Some big names have appeared in the BBC show over the years

    Death in Paradise has been a TV favourite for many years and fans were overjoyed to hear that not only are the cast and crew back filming for series 11, but the show is releasing its first-ever Christmas special at the end of this year.

    Its loyal fanbase has loved its gripping storylines, scenic backdrop and likeable detectives throughout the series' ten year run, but the show is also known for its many cameo appearances from some big names in the film and TV industry.  In fact, viewers may have forgotten that one of the stars of hit US sitcom Friends even made an appearance!

    Helen Baxendale, who played Emily Waltham during seasons four and five of Friends, appeared in the BBC whodunnit drama in 2014. In episode one of series three, the actress starred as the villain Helen Reid, who stole her sister Sasha Moore's identity after her death and visits the island to attend a university reunion.
