It tells the story of Jin Tae-Kyung, a gamer who gets transported to a martial arts world called Murim, where he can use his gaming skills and abilities to fight against powerful enemies and gain fame and fortune. The series has been serialized online since 2018 and has gained a huge fan base around the world.
