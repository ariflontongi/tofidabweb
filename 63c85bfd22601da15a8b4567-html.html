Gerard Pique and Clara Chia have been in the eye of the storm in recent days. After becoming unwitting protagonists of Shakira's song of the moment, now they are also in the spotlight for a cosmetic change that the young girlfriend of the former Barcelona footballer has had.

The lyrics of the Colombian's not-so-subtle song have turned up the intensity of the attention on the new couple and some have been surprised by their findings. It appears that Chia has had some cosmetic surgery, allegedly paid for by the former Barcelona defender.

As reported by the Spanish journalist Jordi Martin of 'Socialite', Pique footed the bill of Clara Chia's first ever cosmetic operation.

Clara Chia's aesthetic touch-up on her face

The aforementioned journalist has assured the world the surgery that Clara Chia has undergone is merely a small retouching on the lips. Apparently, the results can already be seen in the latest photos of the young woman.

Jordi Martin has released this information through a harsh letter addressed to Pique in which the former footballer does not exactly come out very well:

"Many people support you, but I still think what I predicted 12 years ago. That this relationship was too big for you. That you had an inferiority complex with Shakira. You felt tiny next to her," wrote Martin.

"You needed a 23-year-old girl to laugh at you. You are immature. Shakira doesn't need anyone to pay for her cosmetic touch-ups.

"I think you paid for Clara's lips a few days ago, didn't you? You had to pull strings to hire Clara in your company and put her in a position that even her colleagues at Kosmos don't understand."
