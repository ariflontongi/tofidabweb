Bella Hadid Wears a White Di Petsa Wet Look Dress and Yeezys

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Bella Hadid is sharing more of the sexy looks she's sporting on vacation in Ibiza.

The supermodel posted a series of photos of herself posing in a sheer mini dress outside of what looks like her vacation villa. In the photos, Hadid pairs a Di Petsa "Wet Look" halter mini dress with white Yeezy Foam Runners. She accessorized the monochromatic look with yellow-gold jewelry including avant-garde hoops, thin bangles and a large locket adorned with gemstones on a gold chain.

Hadid wore her hair in a messy braid and sported dewy makeup as she posed with large tree branches and vines. She captioned the post, "🕊🌠Do U know how much I love you?"

Hadid has also been sharing the bikinis and beachwear she's been rocking on her vacation. So far she has worn a sunset-print bikini and sarong, paired with a '90s belly chain and bandana, and a bright-red bikini with gold chain adornments by designer Dilara Findikoglu. The 24-year-old paired the latter look with matching cherry-colored Chanel reading glasses, and accessorized with matching gold heart-shaped earrings and necklace.

Hadid, who began her modeling career at 17, recently opened up about joining the industry at such a young age and feeling pressure to project a certain image in a Vogue interview. “It’s like there were two Bellas—me, this person in the process of figuring out who she was, and ‘Bella Hadid’ the alter ego, who was, I dunno, a sexbot who goes out every night?"

"I have insane social anxiety!" she continued. "Partying is not my thing, but I felt enormous pressure to project that image because I assumed that’s all people wanted from me. Now I don’t want to live in that box. I definitely feel like I’m allowed to speak.”
